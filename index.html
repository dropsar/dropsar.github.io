Drops is a Brooklyn-based company specializing in augmented reality (AR), led by CEO/CTO Seth, a seasoned software engineer with 20+ years of experience. Alex, the head of 3D design, holds an MFA and is pursuing a PhD in Human Centered Computing. And Jonathan, Business Development Manager, who's also a Techstars alumn. Advisors include, Sarah Fierek and Frank Woodworth.

Together they aim to create unique AR experiences that focus on creating purpose through 3D, audio and visual rewards. The diverse expertise in technology, partnerships, marketing, and a commitment to social impact positions Drops as an innovative force in the AR industry.

We Specialize in Location-based Experiences
Scavenger hunts, Hide and Seek, Memorials, Geocaching, Immersive Portals, Proof of Participation Rewards, Interactive Murals
Drops Gallery

Drops has helped many of the most creative music agencies (Warner, Atlantic, UMG, Elektra, HBO) and their artists to execute AR experiences to the delight of their fans and followers.

Artists have included Ashnikko, Soccer Mommy, Trivium, Amon Amarth, & HBO.

Each personalized experience utilizes our  web-based AR platform in a number of ways, including scavenger hunts and portals, to provide unique promotional adventures for fans around the globe.
