Our Mission
Drops is an Augmented Reality + Spatial Marketing platform that enables brands and artists to share next-level AR experiences with their fans and followers. At Drops, our mission is to deliver highly memorable, readily accessible, web based augmented reality (AR) experiences to everyone. Let's chat!
Case Study

The Project

Ghost, a wildly popular rock band, and their label, Loma Vista Records, hired Drops to create a location-based experience to promote their first feature length film, “Rite Here Rite Now”.

The Execution

Drops was tasked with geofencing exclusive content at 1800 movie theaters worldwide.

  • This included a landing page with exclusive video content, tickets and merch.
  • Before the release, we displayed a countdown clock to build excitement.
  • Drops simply ingested a spreadsheet of theaters to create the custom set of locations.

The Results

Our data captured a whopping 13% conversion of fans collecting the Drop in person. We estimate this generated upwards of $200k in ticket sales and skyrocket the film to top 10 at the box office worldwide!
